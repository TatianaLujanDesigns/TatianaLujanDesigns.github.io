What I do

I have quite a few things that I have done for the last 5 years that has expanded my knowledge. I started out strictly doing Web design back in 2012, from there I have had the opportunity to learn a little more in the design field. Here is a break down on what I know.

  • Web Design

    Web Design was always the first thing I wanted to go into since high school. Now I know that there is so much more I can do going into this field.

  • Graphic Design

    Graphic Design is what I went into next. Moving schools and learning that they had combined both graphic and web together worked out in my advantage. I now have more experience in both fields.

  • Mock ups

    Mock ups are something that I have improved throughout the years. Normally I use the adobe program InDesign but recently started using Adobe XD.

  • Wire Frames

    Wire Frames are something that I also have improved throughout the years. These are what helped me improve my designs, drawing them out over and over.

  • User Reaserch

    User Reaserch is something that I just learned. I do this by creating personas and then having them go through my mock ups telling me what can be improved. I also create surveys for them to fill out.

  • Illustrative work

    Illustation is something I did when I started learning more about Graphic Design. Normally using Illustrator or Photoshop for my designs.
