Welcome

Thousand Palms Jesus Lives Christian Church is Reaching the Coachella Valley for Christ. God loves each and every person and our mission is to tell everyone about the love of Christ we do this by reaching out to our community. We'd like to invite you to join us for one of our weekly services in Thousand Palms. Check out our Calendar-Service Times. We serve the Coachella Valley by giving the Word of God and by giving food boxes, toys and gifts to families in need. If you would like to partner with us in this ministry and in helping those in need you please contact us or you can donate here online. God Bless you and thank you.
